# Signup

### Create a new account in Signature

- Go to Our app link
- Click ‘Sign up’
  <img src="../../admin/login.png" />
- Please enter Contact person details and organisation details with strong Password. Then click signup now you got like below image.
  <img src="../../admin/signup-verify-email-success.png" />
- Go to given mail id and open Welcome to Signature: Please verify your email address. Click confirm email button or click given link.
  <img src="../../admin/confirm-email-button.png" />
- Email is verified automatically  redirected to <a href="login">**Login**</a> page.
